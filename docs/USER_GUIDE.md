# 2026 Bridges Gala Sponsor Pledge Form - User Guide

Welcome to the 2026 Bridges Gala Sponsor Pledge Form! This guide will help you complete your sponsorship pledge.

---

## Getting Started

Visit the pledge form at: **https://pledge.bridgestowork.org**

---

## Step 1: Select Your Sponsorship Level

Choose from the following sponsorship tiers:

| Level | Investment | Guests Included | Reception Type |
|-------|------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Champion** | $75,000 | 30 guests | VIP Reception |
| **Premier** | $50,000 | 20 guests | VIP Reception |
| **Leadership Circle** | $35,000 | 10 guests | VIP Reception |
| **Partner** | $17,500 | 10 guests | General Reception |
| **Benefactor** | $12,500 | 10 guests | General Reception |
| **Individual Ticket** | $1,500 each | 1 per ticket | General Reception |
| **Donation** | Any amount | N/A | Tax-deductible gift |

### Viewing Benefits

- Click the **info icon** (i) next to any tier to see detailed benefits
- Click **"View Sponsorship Levels & Benefits"** to compare all tiers side-by-side

### Individual Tickets

If you select "Individual Ticket," you can specify the number of tickets you wish to purchase using the quantity selector.

### Donations

If you select "Donation," enter your custom donation amount. All donations are fully tax-deductible.

---

## Step 2: Enter Your Contact Information

### Required Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Company / Organization** | Your company or organization name |
| **Sponsorship Name** | How you'd like to be recognized in event materials (defaults to company name) |
| **Primary Contact** | Name of the main contact person |
| **Street Address** | Mailing address for correspondence |
| **City** | City |
| **State** | Select your state from the dropdown |
| **ZIP** | ZIP or postal code |
| **Email** | Email address for confirmation and communications |
| **Phone** | Phone number (will be formatted automatically) |

### Optional Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Job title of the primary contact |
| **Payment Contact Name** | If someone other than the primary contact handles payment |
| **Payment Contact Email** | Email for the payment contact |

---

## Step 3: Select Payment Method

Choose how you would like to fulfill your pledge:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Check** | Mail a check payable to "Bridges from School to Work" |
| **Credit Card** | Pay online via gala.bridgestowork.org |
| **Wire or Stock Transfer** | Contact the Gala team to arrange |
| **Request an Invoice** | An invoice will be sent to you |

---

## Step 4: Review and Submit

Before submitting, review your **Pledge Summary** which shows:

- Sponsorship Level
- Total Pledge Amount
- Company / Organization
- Sponsorship Name (how you'll be recognized)
- Payment Method

Click **"Submit Pledge"** when ready.

---

## After You Submit

### What Happens Next

1. **Confirmation Page** - You'll be redirected to a thank-you page
2. **Confirmation Email** - You'll receive an email with:
   - Your pledge details
   - A PDF copy of your pledge
   - Payment instructions based on your selected method
3. **Gala Team Follow-Up** - Our team will contact you to coordinate:
   - Payment arrangements
   - Guest list management
   - Sponsorship benefits and recognition

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change my sponsorship level after submitting?

Yes! Contact the Gala team at BridgesGala@orrgroup.com to make changes to your pledge.

### What if I need to update my contact information?

Contact the Gala team with your updated information.

### Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Bridges from School to Work is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (Federal ID# 52-1655740). Your contribution is tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

### When is payment due?

Payment is requested prior to the event on June 22, 2026. The Gala team will work with you on timing.

### Can I split my sponsorship payment?

Contact the Gala team to discuss payment arrangements.

### What if I accidentally close the form before submitting?

If you have entered information, you'll see a warning before leaving the page. Simply return to https://pledge.bridgestowork.org to start again.
